P1.3 // USER SCENARIO MAPPING

ACTOR: Angie Ramos

MOTIVATOR: Angie is craving delicious donuts

INTENTION: Angie saw an post on her feed about donuts

ACTION: Angie searches and customizes her donuts on the Oso Donuts website

RESOLUTION: Angie orders her donuts and picks them up at the storefront

Angie Ramos has been craving donuts for a long time. She recently stumbled across a picture
on her social media feed about Oso Donuts and their unique customizable options. Angie browses the
website and creates and customizes her own donuts. She has a fun time choosing flavors and toppings.
Angie orders the donuts and picks them up at her local storefront.
